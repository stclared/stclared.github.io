About DJ Avery

A Liberian story told through drums, bass and love.

DJ Avery is a Liberian born creative, retired U S Army veteran, author and DJ. From the streets of Buchanan and Monrovia to stages in the United States, he blends Afrobeat, Amapiano, Hipco and roots reggae into one signature sound.

The music is bigger than entertainment. It is memory, healing and celebration. Every track carries a piece of home and a vision for where Africa is going next.

Whether it is a wedding, reunion, club night or festival, DJ Avery brings energy, discipline and love for the crowd. No lazy sets. No empty playlists.

Highlights
  • Author of “Entangled Hearts: A Story of Passion, Lies and Deceit”
  • Retired U S Army, bringing military precision to every show
